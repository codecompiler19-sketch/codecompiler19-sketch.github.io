Delete Your Account - Women Fitness Pro

If you would like to delete your Women Fitness Pro account and associated data, you may request account deletion at any time by following the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Step 1: Send an email to: codecompiler19@gmail.com

Step 2: Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Women Fitness Pro"

Step 3: Include the email address associated with your account.

What Happens Next?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number) will be removed.
  • Your workout history and app usage data will be deleted.

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited period if required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes. Retained data will not be used for advertising or marketing.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7–14 business days.
