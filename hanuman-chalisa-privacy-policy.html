Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Shri Hanuman Chalisa.

Information Collection and Use

The Shri Hanuman Chalisa app is designed for devotional purposes and does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The app does not require user registration, login, or account creation.

Users can read and listen to devotional content including:

  • Hanuman Chalisa (Read and Listen)
  • Hanuman Chalisa Meaning
  • Hanuman Aarti
  • Bajrang Baan
  • Sankat Mochan Hanuman Ashtak
  • Hanuman Mantras
  • Save Favorite Prayers
  • Daily Devotion Reminder

These features work locally on the user's device and do not require personal data.

Advertising

This app may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect anonymous information such as:

  • Device type
  • Advertising ID
  • Basic usage data

This information is used only for the purpose of displaying relevant advertisements.

Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for more information:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children’s Privacy

This application is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, no personal user data is stored, transmitted, or shared by this application.
